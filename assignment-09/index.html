1980s Neon

Neon is not the only noble gas used to produce those trademark glowing
colors we see when we look at "neon" lights. In addition to neon, which
produces a soothing orange-red wavelength, krypton, argon, xeon, and helium
are other noble gases used to produce different colors.


Noble Gas Color Emitted
Helium
Pinkish Red
Neon
Orange Red
Argon
Light Blue
Krypton
Yellow White
Xenon
Bright Lavendar 
The noble gases are excited when they are exposed to moving electrons and
release their corresponding wavelengths of light when they de-excite.
