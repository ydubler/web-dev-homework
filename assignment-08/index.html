Instructable: Cheese Quesadilla

  1. Get your ingredients:
    • Tortilla
    • Cheese
    • Olive Oil
    • Anything else you like

  2. Add a single teaspoon of oil to your pan.



  3. Let your pan (with oil) heat up for three minutes on medium heat.



  4. While the oil is heating up in the pan, cut the cheese into little slabs:



  5. Once the oil has been heated up, add the tortilla to the pan, rotating it so that the oil is coating its underside evenly.



  6. Add the slabs of cheese to half of the tortilla.



  7. Add the other ingredients as you see fit.
  8. After about three to five minutes, after the cheese has melted, fold the quesadilla in half.



  9. Remove the quesadilla from the pan and serve.
