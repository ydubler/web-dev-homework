Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flower
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup butter, softened

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine butter, white sugar, and brown sugar and mix until smooth.
  3. Beat eggs in, one at a time.
  4. Stir in vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add baking soda and salt to batter.
  6. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  7. When adequately mixed, drop large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes until edges are browned nicely.
