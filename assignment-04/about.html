About the Author

T. S. Eliot was born in St. Lous, Missouri on September 26, 1888. He was a poet, essayist, publisher, playwright, literary critic, and editor. He moved to England in 1914 at 25 and became a British subject, renouncing his American citizenship at the age of 39. His poem "The Love Song of J. Alfred would die on January 4th, 1965 in Kensington.
